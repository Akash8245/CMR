Kristu Jayanti College

Kristu Jayanti College (popularly known as KJC) was established in 1999. Kristu Jayanti College is a private-autonomous institution located in Bangalore, Karnataka. The college is affiliated with Bengaluru North University and is accredited by NAAC with an 'A++' Grade.

CMR University

CMR University Bangalore is a private university that was established under Act 45. It is located in Bangalore and was established in 2013. The university is ranked 10 by the Green Institutional Ranking as the Sustainable Institution of India for 2022 and is ISO certified. CMR University has been ranked 2 in the Emerging Engineering Institutes Research Capability category at the Times All India Engineering Institutes Ranking Survey 2024.

MVJ College

MVJ College of Engineering was established in 1982 as the flagship institution of the Venkatesha Education Society to impart education in engineering, management, and beyond. MVJCE’s 15-acre campus in Whitefield, Bangalore, bears state-of-the-art infrastructure that creates an environment for progressive learning and development.

St Joseph University

St Josephs University is a Jesuit university at the heart of Bengaluru, the silicon city of India. Established in 1882 by Paris Foreign Mission Fathers, the management of the college was handed over to the Jesuit order (Society of Jesus) in 1937. The college was first affiliated with the University of Madras and later with the Mysore and Bangalore universities. In 1986, St Joseph’s College became the first affiliated college in Karnataka to offer postgraduate programmes. In 1988, it became the first college in Karnataka to get a Research Centre, and in 2005, it was one of five colleges in Karnataka that was awarded academic autonomy.

RV College

RV College of Engineering Bangalore is a self-financing engineering college managed by the Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust. RVCE is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University and accredited by the NBA. The college is approved by AICTE and the Government of Karnataka. Located 13 km from the heart of Bangalore (also known as the Silicon Valley of India) on Mysore Road. RVCE has ranked 99 in the Engineering category of NIRF Rankings 2024.

Ramaiah College

Ramaiah Institute of Technology Bangalore is ranked 21st by NIRF 2024 under the Architecture and Planning category, 75th under the Engineering category by NIRF 2024, and 5th by India Today 2024 under the Engineering (Private) category. Ramaiah Institute of Technology is an autonomous institute which is affiliated with the Visvesvaraya Technological University. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and the Government of Karnataka. MSRIT is accredited by the NBA and with an A Grade by NAAC.
